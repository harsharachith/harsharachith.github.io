Payroll Management

Every business has to process salary for its employees accurately and timely; however, apart from the statutory and tax regulations (that changes from time to time), managing various salary structures, handling issues like reimbursements, loan advances, leaves, overtime, bonus and other benefits is not an easy task. It may look simple from the outside, but many companies that do their own payroll processing ultimately discovers that it may not be all that cost-effective and simple, especially when you consider the entire payroll management process. For many companies, payroll outsourcing offers an attractive alternative to in-house processing. It provides a simple, affordable way of paying employees, filing taxes, and performing other activities associated with payroll administration. No matter what business you are running and where you are located, we are sure that outsourcing the Payroll management to professional service providers allows you to concentrate in your core activities, reduce your costs and improve the security of your data. RL Associates provides modern and completely customized solutions to all organizations regardless of their size and geographical locations. Our customer tailored payroll solutions directly address payroll management requirements and allows us to build the confidence of our clients to meet their business challenges.

Why You Should Choose RL Associates as Your Payroll Service Provider?
  • To reduce risk of exposure:-  There's a fair amount of risk that comes with in-house payroll. The door is wide open for identity theft and those who handle payroll can view employee records. Employees can tamper with records for personal gain, and there is the risk of embezzlement.
  • To reduce burden on the finance team:- A number of small and medium enterprises generally do not have a specific payroll person. Instead, they assign the responsibility to employees in the HR or accounts department. It might result in inefficiency if the person does not remain updated on legal, tax and other issues.
  • To access a variety of services:- Even a temporary accountant has a limited skill set. With outsourced payroll services in addition to the standard services, companies can also get value-added services like contract generation, labor law compliance, workers' compensation and payments.
  • To save time:- in-house payroll management take too much time and diverts the company from its core operations which can result in the inability of the company to adequately manage and grow its business
  • To decrease the overhead costs:- If one properly thinks about the time spent on payroll tasks, a great deal of cost savings will become evident if these tasks are taken off from the plate.
